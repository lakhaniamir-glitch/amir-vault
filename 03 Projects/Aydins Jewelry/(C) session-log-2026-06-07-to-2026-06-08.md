## Reel Pipeline Work

### Smart-crop centering (for future videos)
Source videos sometimes have the ring off-center in frame. The previous center-crop just took geometric center, missing the subject. Patched `reel_pending_watcher.py` to use ffmpeg `cropdetect` for content-aware cropping.

- Helper: `detect_content_center(src)` runs cropdetect on first 3s
- Helper: `build_centered_crop_filter(src, w, h)` computes x/y offset
- Safety fallback to geometric center if detection fails or content area <40%
- Applied only to future videos (not backfill)
