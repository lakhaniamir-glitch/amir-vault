## Concept summary

Show the transformation from a plain wedding band into a personal keepsake. This branches from the winning Couples Engraved angle without copying the exact same creative.

## Visual direction

Frame 1: clean plain ring on neutral surface.
Frame 2: engraving process close-up, hands or tool visible if real footage is available.
Frame 3: finished engraving readable but not too private.
Frame 4: ring being worn, natural hand shot.

Lighting should be warm, macro-focused, and real. Avoid cutout product stills on white backgrounds.
