## Process (The Aydins Jewelry Workflow)

Every product moves through these 10 steps. The folder structure groups them into 5 phases.

### Phase 1 — Ideas & Validation (folder: `01 Ideas & Validation/`)

**1. Opportunity / Idea spotted**
Sources: supplier product, competitor trend, customer demand, search trends, seasonal angle, lost-sales gap, new customization idea.

**2. Validate the opportunity**
Before doing too much work:
- Is there real demand?
- Can we price it profitably?
- Does it fit the brand?
- Is it different enough from what's already out there?
- Can we improve the offer, listing, or presentation better than competitors?

### Phase 2 — Build (folder: `02 Build/`)

**3. Gather product inputs** — specs, materials, sizes, finishes, supplier photos, cost & margin, production/fulfillment details, engraving/customization options, sellable angle.

**4. Build the offer** — product name, target customer, pricing, value proposition, warranty/fit/engraving messaging, what makes it special.

### Phase 3 — Assets (folder: `03 Assets/`)

**5. Create the assets** — product photos, alt/lifestyle images, video if needed, title, description, SEO title, meta description, keywords, tags, alt text, badges/trust messaging.

### Phase 4 — Launch (folder: `04 Launch/`)

**6. Publish the listing** — Shopify product setup, variants & sizing, pricing, images in right order, collections, shipping details, metafields/SEO, mobile check, conversion-ready page.

**7. Push it into channels** — Etsy if relevant, Google Merchant Center, organic social, email/SMS, paid ads, homepage/collections/internal links, seasonal placement.

*"Live" is not the same as "launched." A listing without a traffic plan is orphaned.*

### Phase 5 — Performance & Iteration (folder: `05 Performance & Iteration/`)

**8. Track performance** — traffic, CTR, add-to-cart rate, conversion rate, ROAS, ranking/impressions, customer questions, returns/sizing issues, interested-but-not-buying signal.

**9. Iterate fast** — better photos, stronger title, better opening hook, different pricing, trust messaging, ad creative, personalization angle, SEO/tags.

**10. Scale or cut** — proven winners get more traffic, more creatives, harder feature placement, related products, reorder. Losers get reworked or killed.

### Aydins Jewelry — Core operating rules
- **Always include pricing math before greenlighting a product** — cost, shipping, platform fees, ad tolerance, target margin. No product moves forward without it.
- **Never publish a listing without a mobile check.**
- **Every product must have complete SEO fields** — title, meta title, meta description, focus keywords, alt text.
- **Never launch with weak or incomplete imagery.** If the photos are not good enough to sell, the listing is not ready.
- **Every product needs a clear angle** — who it's for, why it's different, why someone buys this one instead of another.
- **No listing goes live without checking variants, sizes, pricing, and shipping settings.**
- **If it's customizable, personalization instructions must be crystal clear.**
- **Every launch needs a traffic plan** — organic, email/SMS, ads, Etsy, Google, or internal site placement. "Live" is not the same as "launched."
- **Track performance after launch. No orphan listings.**
- **Every opportunity evaluation must include competitive references and what we're doing better.**

### Listing copy rules (white-label + voice)
- **Never mention another brand** in any listing, SEO field, meta description, alt text, or marketing copy. Aydins Jewelry white-labels wholesale rings (sourced from Thorsten via universal-jewelry.com). Every product is presented as handcrafted / made by Aydins Jewelry — no Thorsten, JCK, universal-jewelry, or third-party brand names anywhere customer-facing.
- **Shopify `vendor` field is internal-only** — leave existing vendor values (e.g. "Universal J") as-is. The vendor field is not displayed on the storefront, so it doesn't violate the white-label rule. Do NOT change vendor on existing products.
- **Comfort fit is default** — all Aydins rings are comfort fit unless specified otherwise. Always list in Key Features.
- **Customer protection — see [[(C) Aydins Policies — Source of Truth]] for the complete current policy.** All listings, emails, ads, and any customer-facing copy must use the verified trust pillars in that file. **Never write "lifetime warranty," "free lifetime resizing," or "30-day free returns" as customer-facing claims** — those phrases are fabrications, not actual policy. The real policy is more nuanced (paid programs, restocking fees, exclusions for engraved items) — and the truth, told well, is more impressive than the lie.
- **Tone** — polished, masculine, high-end, direct, conversion-focused. Short paragraphs. Mobile-friendly. Key Features in a bolded bullet list. Close with a short lifestyle/emotional line.
- **No em dashes (`—`) in any Aydins content.** Locked rule, 2026-05-15. Use periods, commas, colons, semicolons, or parens instead. Applies to: customer-facing copy (listings, emails, SMS, popups, ads, packaging inserts), internal specs Claude writes, and any new files created in this project. Existing files with em dashes will be cleaned on touch (when next edited); a full historical sweep is a separate task. Em dashes are tolerated only in: (a) accurate filename references where renaming would break wikilinks, and (b) the rule itself showing the banned character.

### Engraving convention (Zepto personalizer)
- **Source-driven logic** — check the wholesale (universal-jewelry.com) source page:
  - Source says "Engravable In Only" → **inside-only engraving**
  - Source says "Inside and Outside" → **both inside and outside engraving**
  - General rule: any ring with outside inlay = inside-only
- **Shopify tags drive Zepto UI** (add the tag exactly, NO parentheses):
  - Inside-only engraving → tag: `Inside`
  - Inside + Outside engraving → tag: `Inside & Outside`
- ⚠️ Verified 2026-05-07: Zepto reads the bare-word tags (no parens). Earlier draft of this rule had parens — wrong.
- Listing copy must mention engraving availability in Key Features and in the emotional close ("Engrave the inside, make it his").

### Hard "never" rules
- No publishing half-finished listings
- No skipping pricing math
- No weak first image
- No "we'll fix SEO later"
- No product launches without knowing the next traffic source
- No third-party brand names in customer-facing copy

### SEO length rules (Google guidelines — hard limits)
- **Meta / SEO page title: ≤70 characters** (Google truncates around 60 in SERP; 70 is the outer ceiling)
- **Meta description: ≤150 characters** (Google truncates around 155 on mobile; 150 is the safe ceiling)
- Front-load the primary keyword. Brand name goes at the end if it fits ("| Aydins" — 8 chars including separator).
- Count every character including spaces and punctuation. When in doubt, shorter beats over-budget.
- Meta description should still communicate: material + width + key feature + trust signal (comfort fit / lifetime warranty / handcrafted).

### Variant sizing convention
- **Only use the sizes listed on Universal Jewelry (source) for each width.** Do not extend or shrink the size range. Some rings have multiple widths with different size ranges per width — respect that exactly.
- **Inventory default: 10 per size** (with `tracked: true` on every inventory item so totalInventory counts properly).

### Opportunity evaluation checklist
Every opportunity must answer these before moving out of `01 Ideas & Validation/`:
1. What is the product?
2. Why now?
3. Who is it for?
4. What are competitors doing?
5. What makes ours stronger?
6. What is the landed cost?
7. What selling price makes sense?
8. What margin do we keep?
9. What assets do we already have?
10. What still needs to be made?
11. How will we launch it?
12. How will we judge success?

### Listing completion standard
A listing is only "done" if it includes:
- Final title
- Product description
- Key features
- SEO title
- Meta description
- Focus keywords
- Image alt text
- Pricing
- Variant setup
- Personalization details (if applicable)
- Trust/value messaging
- Mobile check complete

### Launch convention
Before anything is marked live:
- Site listing published
- Collections assigned
- Images checked
- SEO filled
- Mobile checked
- Links tested
- Tracking in place
- Launch channel selected
- First promotion plan ready

### Performance convention
Every live product gets a simple review log:
- Launch date
- Traffic source
- Conversion rate
- Add-to-cart rate
- Sales
- Feedback / issues
- Next action
