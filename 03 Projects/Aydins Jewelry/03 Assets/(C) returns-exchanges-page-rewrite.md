# Returns & Exchanges

Your complete satisfaction is our priority. Every Aydins Jewelry purchase is backed by a hassle-free **30-day return and exchange policy** — plus a **lifetime warranty** and **lifetime sizing** for the original purchaser.

We've made the process simple. Start your return or exchange in our self-service portal in under 60 seconds — no printed forms, no phone calls, no waiting on email replies.

## Start a Return or Exchange

**[→ Open the Returns Portal](https://aydins.thunderreturns.com)**

Log in with your order number and email. The portal will walk you through:

1. **Choose your order** — pick the ring you want to return or exchange.
2. **Tell us what you'd like to do** — exchange for a different size, a different style, or request a refund.
3. **Print your packing slip** — the portal generates a packing slip with your request details. Print it and include it in the package with your ring.
4. **Ship it back to us** — using the carrier of your choice, send the package to the mailing address below. Customer is responsible for return shipping. Once we receive your ring, your refund or replacement is processed within 3–5 business days.

That's it. You can track the status of your request inside the portal at any time.

## Policy Overview

### Exchanges (within 30 days, no engraving)

- **Free exchange** — no fee on standard tungsten, ceramic, cobalt, and titanium rings.
- **Custom 14k gold rings:** $100 exchange fee.
- **Shipping:** customer covers outbound; Aydins Jewelry covers return shipping for US orders.
- **Condition:** ring must be unworn, unengraved, and in original condition.

### Exchanges (engraved or after 30 days) — covered by Lifetime Sizing & Warranty

Engraved rings and rings exchanged after the 30-day window fall under our **Lifetime Sizing & Warranty Program**, available to the original purchaser:

- **$34.50** if exchanged within the first year
- **$54.50** after the first year — flat fee for the lifetime of the ring
- **Custom 14k gold rings:** flat $100 fee
- Replacement ring must match the original style and width.
- Customer covers outbound shipping; Aydins Jewelry covers return shipping for US orders.

### Returns

- **30-day window** from the delivery date.
- **$25 restocking fee** on standard rings.
- **25% restocking fee** on custom 14k gold rings.
- **Original shipping costs are deducted** from the refund amount.
- **Engraved rings cannot be returned** — but they are fully covered by Lifetime Sizing.
