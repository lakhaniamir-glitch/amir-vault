## Aydins Protection Plan (paid add-on at checkout — primarily shipping insurance)

Source: https://shopaydins.com/pages/aydins-protection-plan-terms-conditions

**Plain-language summary:** This is a shipping insurance product, not a ring warranty. The ring breakage / defect coverage above is already free for every customer. The Protection Plan adds protection against things that happen *during shipping* — package lost in transit, damaged in transit, stolen on the doorstep — plus a couple of bonus perks.

**Cost (tiered by cart total):**
| Cart total | Plan price |
|---|---|
| $0–50 | $9.75 |
| $50–100 | $19.75 |
| $100–499 | $29.75 |
| $499–749 | $49.75 |
| $749+ | $99.75 |

**What the plan ADDS over the default policy (i.e., why a customer would buy it):**
- **Shipping loss / damage protection** — package never arrives, or arrives damaged, replaced free of charge
- **Theft protection** — package shows FedEx delivery to the correct address but is stolen
- **One-time free sizing replacement on engraved items within 30 days** — waives the standard $34.50 engraved-sizing fee

**What's NOT covered (even with the plan):**
- Intentional damage
- Normal wear and tear (finish dulling, daily-use scratches)
- Theft without FedEx proof of delivery to the correct address

**Duration:** 6 months from purchase. After 6 months, the customer falls back to the default Lifetime Defect / Breakage Coverage program (see section above) — same as everyone.

**Claim process:**
- Damaged items must be returned for inspection before a replacement is sent
- Theft claims require FedEx proof of delivery to the correct address
- Shipping loss claims: confirmation of non-delivery from FedEx

**Eligibility:** Customer adds the Protection Plan as a separate product to cart at checkout. **Not automatic on any order.**

✅ Approved phrasing:
- (Pre-purchase / on PDP / in checkout flow): "Add Aydins Protection at checkout — covers your ring against loss, damage, or theft in shipping for 6 months."
- (Pre-purchase): "Worried about shipping? Add Aydins Protection at checkout — for $9.75–$99.75 we cover loss, damage, and theft for 6 months."
- (Post-purchase, only to customers who actually bought it): "Your Aydins Protection Plan covers you against loss, damage, or theft for 6 months. Reply to this email if anything happens with your shipment."

❌ Do NOT phrase as:
- "Built-in protection" (it's a paid add-on, not built-in)
- "All Aydins rings come with shipping protection" (false unless they bought the plan)
- "We replace lost packages free" (only true if they bought the plan)
- "Theft covered" (only true if they bought the plan AND have FedEx proof of delivery)
