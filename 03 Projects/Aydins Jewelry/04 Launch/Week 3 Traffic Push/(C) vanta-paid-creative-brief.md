# (C) VANTA — Paid Creative Brief

**Campaign:** Week 3 Traffic Push — Paid Test
**Product:** VANTA — 14k Gold Black Carbon Fiber Wedding Ring, 8mm
**Channel recommendation:** **Meta Advantage+ Shopping** (faster creative iteration + better cold-audience reach for textured/visual products). Google Performance Max is a secondary option once Meta creative is proven.
**Landing page:** https://aydinsjewelry.com/products/14k-yellow-gold-ring-with-black-carbon-fiber-inlay-beveled-edge-wedding-band-polished-design
**Budget:** $50/day × 7 days = $350 total test
**Goal:** Validate cold-traffic interest in premium 14k carbon-fiber ring + unlock scalable creative angle.

---

## Why VANTA for the paid test

- **Highest-margin ring in the upgraded catalog** — solid 14k gold, not tungsten. Wider ad tolerance.
- **Clearest, most defensible story** — "real aerospace-grade carbon fiber in solid gold" is unique, not a commodity tungsten SEO play.
- **Targetable buyer personas** — engineers, architects, F1 fans, pilots, watch collectors.
- **Creative-ready** — hero images shot in 3 metals (yellow/white/rose gold), lifestyle shots already in PDP.

---

## Ad Hooks (3–5 variations)

1. **"Formula 1 chassis. In a wedding band."** — intrigue/credibility. Performance/aerospace angle.
2. **"Real carbon fiber. Solid 14k gold. Not printed. Not painted."** — proof/anti-fake angle. Direct.
3. **"Most wedding bands are forgettable. This one has a weave pattern."** — contrast/identity angle.
4. **"For the guy who notices how things are made."** — persona hook. Hits engineers/designers/makers.
5. **"Five times stronger than steel. At a fraction of the weight."** — material spec angle. Works with macro shots.

## Creative Direction

### Static — 3 variations (1:1 + 4:5 crops)

**Static 1 — Hero three-metals (use existing `canary-vanta/hero-three-metals.png`)**
- All three finishes (yellow / white / rose) side-by-side on neutral stone.
- Headline overlay (bottom-third): **"Three metals. One weave."**
- Small CTA badge: **Shop Now**.

**Static 2 — Macro weave detail**
- Tight crop on the carbon fiber inlay so the diagonal weave pattern is visible.
- Minimal type overlay: **"Not printed. Real."**
- Corner badge: **14k Gold · Lifetime Warranty**.

**Static 3 — Lifestyle on-hand**
- Hand shot in context: tailored cuff, mechanical watch visible, VANTA on ring finger.
- Copy overlay (top): **"For the man who notices how things are made."**

### Video — 1 concept, 9–15 seconds (1:1 and 9:16)

**Concept: "Proof" — 15-second cut**
- 0–2s: Black screen → white text fades in: **"Most wedding bands are painted black."**
- 2–4s: Quick macro pan across the VANTA's carbon-fiber inlay. Light catches diagonal weave.
- 4–6s: White text: **"This one isn't."**
- 6–9s: Rotating 360° of the ring on a neutral stand. Yellow gold. White text: **"Authentic woven carbon fiber."**
- 9–12s: Quick-cut three metals (yellow → white → rose). White text: **"Solid 14k. Three finishes."**
- 12–15s: Hand shot (ring on finger, watch visible). Final card: **"VANTA. Aydins Jewelry."** + CTA button.

Keep it silent-first (captions burned in). Music: minimal/mechanical, low tempo — think watch-brand ad pacing, not jewelry-store pacing.

### Notes
- No third-party brand names in frame (no F1 logos, no aerospace branding). "F1 chassis" and "aerospace-grade" live in copy only.
- Keep copy under 15% of image area (Meta relaxed the 20% rule but performance still favors clean visuals).
- All creatives must show the ring on a hand at least once in the set — cold traffic needs scale reference.

## Budget & Pacing

---

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Kill Threshold |
|---|---|---|
| CTR (link) | ≥ 1.5% | < 0.8% |
| ROAS | ≥ 2.0 | < 1.0 after day 5 |
| Add-to-cart rate (LP) | ≥ 5% | < 2% |
| CPC | ≤ $1.50 | > $3.00 |
| CPA | ≤ $80 | > $150 |

**Scale criteria (if hit by day 7):**
- Bump budget to $100/day, add 2 new creative variations on winning angle.
- Clone to Google PMax using same feed + assets.
- Consider pushing to SMS/email retargeting for site visitors who didn't convert.

**Iterate criteria (if CTR ok but ROAS low):**
- LP isn't closing — revisit hero image / price anchor / trust badges.
- Test free-shipping badge on product card.
- Consider bundle upsell (matching women's band) in ad copy.

**Kill criteria (if below all targets by day 7):**
- Cold traffic isn't resonating with carbon-fiber-in-gold story at this price point.
- Pivot paid test to a $169 tungsten anchor (COMET or ARBORIX) and re-brief.
