02THE THOR ANCHOR

When in doubt about a creative decision, ask: would Thor own/wear/buy this?
⚡ The Reference Vibe
"Theonar is the brand the god of thunder would buy for himself. Cold metal. Storm energy. Built for the strike, the swing, the grip."
✓ ON BRAND (Thor energy)
Stormy skies, hammered metal textures, lightning-style backdrops, deep black with single bright key light, leather and rope props (Norse but not cosplay), mountain peaks, raw stone, aged bronze detailing as punctuation
✗ OFF BRAND (avoid)
Capes, horned helmets, literal runes, Marvel cosplay, anything sweet/floral/warm/soft, copper/orange dominant tones (that's Hephaestus/forge), white marble, velvet ring boxes
