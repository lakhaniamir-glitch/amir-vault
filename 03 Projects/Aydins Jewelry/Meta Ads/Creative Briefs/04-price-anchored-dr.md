## Concept summary

A direct-response ad that makes the value obvious fast. The final price claim must use a real featured product price before production. Until Amir selects the ring or collection, the ad is drafted as a price-anchored template.

## Visual direction

- Single strong ring image, ideally worn or macro texture.
- Text overlay: `Personalized. Ships fast. Under $X.`
- Secondary proof line: `Free engraving from Aydins.`
- Clean ivory or charcoal background.
- Avoid discount-heavy design and fake urgency.

## Production checklist

- Select one featured product or collection with true price point.
- Confirm price claim, for example under $X, against live PDP.
- Confirm featured product supports engraving through Zepto.
- Choose one strong product image or phone macro.
- Add overlay only after price is confirmed.

## Test plan

- Ship into the existing winning ad set only after Amir approval.
- Kill rule: $30 spent and 0 purchases, or CTR under 1%, or no ATC at $20 spend.
- Success: ROAS above 3.0 within $100 spend.
