# 30-Day Plan — Strengthen The Core

> **Status:** Active plan
> **Start date:** 2026-04-21
> **End date:** 2026-05-21
> **Author:** Amir (user-written, not AI-generated)

---

## 30-Day Priority

**Strengthen the core business: Aydins Jewelry.**

### Why this over the alternatives
Fastest path to cash. Lowest risk. Most likely to reduce stress. The business is already real, already selling, already has history. Finishing a secondary offer or improving systems can matter later — right now they are easier places to hide than to win.

### Main objective
Increase the consistency and performance of Aydins Jewelry by improving the products and channels most likely to make money in the next 30 days.

### What success looks like by Day 30
- 10 to 15 high-priority product listings fully upgraded
- 1 clear traffic push behind those listings
- A simple weekly scorecard in place
- A measurable lift in performance, ideally:
  - +10 to 15% conversion improvement on worked listings, or
  - +10 to 20% revenue lift from the focused product group, or
  - Better add-to-cart rate, click-through rate, or lower drop-off if traffic is the bigger issue

---

## Week 1 — Find the Money

**Goal:** figure out what deserves attention first.

- Pull the last 60 to 90 days of sales and traffic
- Identify:
  - Top 10 best-selling products
  - Top 10 most-viewed but weak-converting products
  - Top 5 products with strong potential but weak presentation
- Pick one core focus group, not the whole catalog:
  - Best sellers needing better conversion, OR
  - High-traffic products needing better listings

**Output by end of Week 1:**
- A shortlist of 10 to 15 SKUs
- A ranked list:
  - Fix first
  - Fix second
  - Ignore for now

---

## Week 2 — Rebuild the Money Pages

**Goal:** make the chosen listings stronger.

For each selected product:
- Rewrite title
- Improve first image
- Improve product description
- Tighten key features
- Add or improve SEO title and meta description
- Add alt text
- Clean up variants / options
- Check personalization instructions
- Verify trust / value messaging is visible
- Do a mobile check

**Do not:**
- Create a bunch of new products this week
- Redesign the whole store
- Drift into side projects

**Output by end of Week 2:**
- At least 5 to 8 listings fully redone
- First image quality improved on all priority products
- SEO fields completed on all updated listings

---

## Week 3 — Push Traffic On Purpose

**Goal:** get eyes on the improved pages.

Choose **one** primary traffic / distribution push:
- Google Merchant / organic shopping
- Email + SMS push
- Paid social creative test
- Homepage / collection placement
- Etsy push if relevant

**Best move for this window:** push existing proven products harder, not random new ones.

**Tasks:**
- Create 3 to 5 fresh creatives for the selected products
- Run 1 email campaign
- Run 1 SMS campaign
- Feature priority listings in the right collections or homepage placements
- Make sure internal linking supports the products

**Output by end of Week 3:**
- Improved listings are not just live — they are actually being pushed
- Traffic source is attached to the work

---

## Week 4 — Review, Cut, Double Down

**Goal:** learn what worked and act on it fast.

**Look at:**
- Sessions
- Click-through rate
- Add-to-cart rate
- Conversion rate
- Revenue by worked SKU
- Return / sizing / customer friction
- Which images or hooks performed best

**Then:**
- Double down on the top 3 winners
- Rewrite or re-image the weak ones
- Cut anything that still looks dead after a fair push

**Output by end of Week 4:**
- 3 winners identified
- 3 underperformers either reworked or deprioritized
- A repeatable "fix → launch → measure" cycle

---

## What NOT to Do for the Next 30 Days

*This matters more than the task list.*

- Do not start a new software build
- Do not launch a new secondary business offer
- Do not spend the month organizing systems instead of selling
- Do not work on low-probability products just because they are interesting
- Do not redesign things that are not directly tied to conversion or sales
