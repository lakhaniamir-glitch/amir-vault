## Voice + Hard Rules Used in Every Rewrite

- 2-4 sentences max. Mobile-friendly.
- No em dashes.
- No banned phrases: no "lifetime warranty" bare, no "free returns," no "free lifetime resizing," no "handcrafted," no "forged," no "Grapevine Mills," no third-party brand names.
- Brand name: **Aydins** (no apostrophe).
- Workshop location: **Irving, Texas**.
- Engraving: **free on every order**.
- Tone: direct, confident, masculine, conversion-focused. No "We'd be happy to help!" customer-service-bot fluff.
- When a question is policy-dependent, include the constraint (fee, window, eligibility) instead of pretending it's free or unlimited.
