## Concept summary

A real customer explains that she did not want his wedding band to feel basic. The ad should feel like a natural phone video or a simple quote card, not polished brand theater. The emotional angle is personal meaning, not jewelry specs.

## Visual direction

- Shot style: vertical phone video, natural light, clean home background.
- Framing: customer from chest up, then cutaway to ring on hand or ring box.
- Lighting: bright window light, warm and real.
- Overlay text: one short quote line only.
- Avoid: fake AI scenes, stock couple footage, overproduced transitions.
