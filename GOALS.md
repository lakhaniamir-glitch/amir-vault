## Scorecard Cadence & Miss Protocol

**Frequency:** Weekly, every Sunday. Tied to [[05 Skills/weekly-update.md]].

**What gets scored weekly:**
- Am I on track toward this quarter's numbers?
- Did I ship something this week, or did I organize?
- Hit ≥20 workouts/month pace? 2 phone-free nights this week? 5 no-work-after-9 nights?
- Anything live today that doesn't belong to Aydins core or Thunder Returns?

**Miss protocol:**
1. Review the miss within **7 days**
2. **Cut scope and re-plan — once.**
3. If the same target or vehicle misses again → **park it.** No blind timeline extensions. No emotional excuse-making.

**What this means in practice:**
- **Aydins** doesn't get abandoned — it's the core vehicle. Misses = simplify, narrow focus, fix execution.
- **Thunder Returns** gets **one re-plan max.** If milestones repeatedly slip or no real customer pull by Dec 31, 2026 → parked.
