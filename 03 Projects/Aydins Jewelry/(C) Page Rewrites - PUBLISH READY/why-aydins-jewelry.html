Our story

One family. One ring at a time.

Aydins started in 2011 inside a single North Texas mall kiosk, one family, a few hundred styles, one promise: sell wedding bands the way they used to be sold, by people who cared what the customer would think when they opened the box.

Fifteen years later, we ship to customers in all 50 states and over 60 countries. We still engrave every order on our own laser. We still answer the phone. The names in the company haven’t changed.

Today the work happens out of our workshop in Irving, Texas, same family, same shop, same standard.

Common questions

Why Aydins, answered.

Is Aydins Jewelry a real jeweler or a drop-shipper?

Real jeweler. We’ve been in business since 2011 and run a workshop in Irving, Texas. Every order is engraved and inspected at our facility before shipping.

Where do your rings ship from?

Materials are sourced globally (tungsten from Asia, gold refined in the U.S., exotic inlays from various origins) and engraving, inspection, and shipping all happen at our shop in Irving, Texas.

How long has Aydins been in business?

Since 2011, over fifteen years. The same family that started it still runs it.

What makes Aydins different from competitors?

Three things: (1) the catalog, over 1,000 styles in one place, including materials most jewelers don’t carry; (2) the included services, free U.S. shipping, free engraving, free 30-day exchange, plus a Lifetime Warranty and Lifetime Sizing program; (3) honest pricing, no flash sales, no fake urgency, no surprise fees.

Can I visit the store?

Our workshop in Irving, Texas is not open to walk-in customers. If you’re local and need help, sizing, a custom build, a fitting, give us a call or email and we’ll work something out.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, via FedEx or UPS Express at flat rates to most countries. Customs and duties are the customer’s responsibility. Some areas and PO Boxes are excluded.
