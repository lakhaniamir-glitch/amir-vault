## Task 2: Klaviyo Email Automation — Full Setup Guide

Amir pays for Klaviyo but barely uses it. Complete implementation guide for every high-impact automated flow.

### Required Flows
1. **Abandoned Checkout** — #1 revenue recovery flow. Full sequence: timing, subject lines, content, discount strategy, # emails.
2. **Abandoned Cart** — for users who add to cart but don't start checkout.
3. **Browse Abandonment** — for users who view products but don't add to cart.
4. **Welcome Series** — for new subscribers (ties into Recart 20% discount popup).
5. **Post-Purchase** — thank you, review request, cross-sell, care instructions per ring material.
6. **Win-Back** — for customers who haven't purchased in 6+ months.
7. **Price Drop / Back in Stock** — if applicable for catalog.

### For Each Flow, Provide
- Trigger conditions
- Number of emails in sequence
- Timing between each email (hours/days)
- Subject lines (3 options per email for A/B testing)
- Email body content — written for men's wedding bands, brand voice (premium, straightforward, no fluff)
- Discount strategy & amount (consider margins: tungsten high, 14k gold lower)
- Klaviyo-specific setup steps (what to click, where in the UI)

### Additional Klaviyo Research
- Features a jewelry store should be using that most owners miss.
- Customer list segments to create.
- Best-performing email templates / layouts for jewelry e-commerce in 2026.
- Properly connecting Klaviyo with Shopify to capture all events (browse, cart, checkout, purchase).
