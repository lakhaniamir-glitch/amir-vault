## Concept summary

This is a small story instead of a product grid. It starts with the emotional problem, shows the personalization feature, adds proof, and closes with a clear shop action.

## Visual direction

Frame 1: hook text over couple/ring shopping moment.
Frame 2: close-up of ring detail and engraving option.
Frame 3: review or customer quote screenshot-style graphic.
Frame 4: finished ring with CTA.

Use dark charcoal, ivory, and warm jewelry tones. Keep text large and minimal.
