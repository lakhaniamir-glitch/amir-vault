If you buy it, you get <strong>one free exchange</strong> within 6 months from the day you receive your ring (size, style, even engraved) with standard exchange fees waived. Without the Plan, the standard exchange schedule applies. Read the <a class="apg-link" href="/pages/aydins-protection-plan-terms-conditions">full Protection Plan terms</a>.</p>
        </div>

          <p>The customer covers return shipping on returns and on standard exchanges. Aydins covers outbound shipping on the replacement ring.</p>
        </div>

          <p>Returns and exchanges are about <em>changing your mind</em>. The <a class="apg-link" href="/pages/lifetime-sizing-lifetime-warranty">Lifetime Warranty</a> is a separate program covering manufacturing defects: free in the first 6 months, $34.50 from 6&ndash;12 months, $54.50 after 12 months. Items priced over $1,000 carry a 25% surcharge at every tier. <a class="apg-link" href="/pages/lifetime-sizing-lifetime-warranty">Lifetime Sizing</a> is a third, separate program, for the original purchaser, small flat fee, no time limit. All three live alongside each other.</p>
