## Why these 5 — one-paragraph rationale

These five rings were picked because each one carries a distinct, story-driven visual angle that stops scroll and survives cold traffic — meteorite pattern, tree bark, Celtic cross, two-tone rose gold, and real carbon fiber in solid gold. They're not variations of the same idea; each earns its own email slot and its own subject-line hook. Four of them (COMET, ARBORIX, GARROS, MARAR) sit at the $169 accessible price point that converts cold list traffic, while VANTA anchors the premium end and doubles as the paid-test hero (highest-margin, most defensible product in the catalog thanks to real 14k gold + authentic aerospace-weave carbon fiber). Together they show the full range of the upgraded catalog without cannibalizing each other — meteorite and tree-bark are both textured but pull different crowds (space/geology vs. outdoors/rugged); Celtic cross pulls meaning-driven buyers; MARAR pulls warm-tone/vintage buyers; VANTA pulls the designer/engineer buyer at the top of the budget. Every pick has completed copy and imagery from the April 2026 revamp, so nothing ships half-finished.
